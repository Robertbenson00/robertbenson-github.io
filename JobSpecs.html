What do I need to join?

  • First of all gain 5 A*-C (9-4), including English and Maths at GCSE.
  • Next take an A Level or BTEC Level 3 in Information Technology or related field (such as computer science or programming)
  • Gain entry into university and get at least a 2:1 in a related field degree.

For details of the qualifications, see below (as stated by main-stream universities):

  • BTEC National Diploma in Art and Design (Multimedia), Computer studies or IT. Applicants typically need GCSEs (Grades A-C/1-3) or equivalent qualifications

  • SQA National Certificate in IT. Applicants normally require Four GCSEs (Grades A-C/1-3)

  • BTEC/SQA Higher National Diplomas. Colleges typically ask for one A level/ Two H grades and 4 GCSEs/S grades (Grades A-C/1-3) or BTEC/SQA National certificate/diploma in relevant subject or course and some colleges offer sandwich courses, with one year's work experience.

  • NQTs/SVQs are available in IT practitioner courses, including design at levels 2 to 4.

  • Degrees in IT, Computer science, programming, software engineering and other subjects. More specific degree subjects include web development, web design, multimedia design and web content management. Applicants usually need two A levels/ Three H grades. Degree courses usually last three or four years full time or four or five years for sandwich courses.
